About this game

In 2009, I made an alphabet game in Flash with my 4-year-old daughter Jane. She loved talking like a robot, so we made a character out of it. The animations were pretty simple, but we were surprised how many people shared it with their kids. With new AI tools available, I thought it was a good chance to bring it back with a fresh coat of paint (and the original audio). I hope you enjoy it!
